About S&S Properties

A personal property business with a professional standard.

S&S Properties is a property management and serviced accommodation business founded and run by Shannon. Our mission is to provide high-quality accommodation for guests while helping property owners maximise the potential of their properties through professional, reliable management.

We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional customer service, attention to detail, and a hands-on approach to every property we manage. Whether it’s providing comfortable serviced accommodation for travellers or taking the stress out of property management for landlords, our focus is on creating value and positive experiences for everyone we work with.

At S&S Properties, we believe in building long-term relationships based on trust, transparency, and results.
